# EXP-ADOPT-017 — Skill Ecosystem

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** V — AI Ecosystem  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Build and publish a growing ecosystem of skills that extend SYNTH agents.

---

## Purpose

Skills are the distribution mechanism for agent capabilities. A rich, well-governed skill catalog increases adoption and makes SYNTH more useful to AI agents.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Skill authoring guide and template**.
2. **Public skill registry schema and repository**.
3. **Initial skill set**: at least five reference skills (e.g., documentation, testing, deployment, governance, design).
4. **Skill versioning and update policy**.
5. **Skill download analytics**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least five skills are published to the registry.
- The skill template passes validation.
- Each skill has a README, tests, and a usage example.
- The registry supports search and version pinning.
- Download telemetry is reported in EXP-ADOPT-019.

---

## Out of Scope

- Skills that bypass governance gates.
- Proprietary skill marketplace.
- Skills requiring non-deterministic execution.
