# EXP-ADOPT-018 — Integration Showcase

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** V — AI Ecosystem  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Demonstrate SYNTH operating with third-party tools and platforms.

---

## Purpose

Third-party integrations prove SYNTH is usable in real engineering stacks. A public showcase turns integrations into adoption evidence.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Integration roadmap and partner criteria**.
2. **At least three reference integrations** (e.g., CI/CD, issue tracker, documentation host).
3. **Integration documentation and deployment examples**.
4. **Showcase page** on the homepage or docs site.
5. **Maintenance and versioning policy** for integrations.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each integration has a working example and documented setup steps.
- The showcase page lists all integrations with status and maintainer.
- Integrations do not compromise the deterministic execution model.
- At least one integration is contributed by someone outside the core team.
- Integration tests run in CI.

---

## Out of Scope

- Official partnerships requiring legal review.
- Integrations that violate protected assets.
- Paid vendor co-marketing.
