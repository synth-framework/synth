# EXP-ADOPT-008 — Documentation Articles

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** II — Launch Assets  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Publish authoritative articles that establish SYNTH's mental models and answer common questions.

---

## Purpose

Articles turn principles and architecture into discoverable, shareable content. They feed the documentation hub, social channels, and AI crawlers.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Editorial calendar** for the first 12 articles.
2. **Article templates** aligned with the brand voice.
3. **Core article set**:
   - Introduction to SYNTH
   - Deterministic execution
   - The Mission lifecycle
   - The governance model
   - SDK quick-start
   - AI agent integration
   - Comparison with other frameworks
4. **Cross-posting plan** to Dev.to, Hashnode, and Medium.
5. **Author attribution** and review checklist.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least eight articles are published and cross-posted.
- Each article has a clear learning outcome.
- All technical claims are linked to evidence (docs, tests, or examples).
- Articles use the approved brand voice.
- At least four articles include runnable code snippets from the examples library.

---

## Out of Scope

- Sponsored content.
- Opinion pieces unrelated to SYNTH.
- Paywalled publications.
