# EXP-ADOPT-004 — Homepage Launch

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** I — Platform Foundations  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Publish the Program 027 homepage as the public entry point for SYNTH.

---

## Purpose

The homepage is the canonical conversion surface. This expedition coordinates the public launch of the approved Mission Studio homepage and ensures it connects seamlessly to docs, repo, and install paths.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Final homepage deployment** to the production host.
2. **SEO metadata, Open Graph tags, and analytics instrumentation**.
3. **Homepage-to-docs and homepage-to-repo navigation**.
4. **Launch smoke tests** across devices and browsers.
5. **Post-launch monitoring and rollback plan**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Homepage loads in less than 3 seconds on a simulated 3G connection.
- All primary calls-to-action route to docs or install.
- No broken links from the homepage to docs or the repository.
- Analytics capture page views and CTA clicks without leaking PII.
- Launch is certified per EXP-ADOPT-020.

---

## Out of Scope

- Redesigning the homepage (owned by EXP-PROGRAM-027).
- Content not approved by EXP-PROGRAM-027.
- Paid promotion.
