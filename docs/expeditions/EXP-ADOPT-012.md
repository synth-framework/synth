# EXP-ADOPT-012 — OSS Contribution Experience

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** III — Developer Education  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Make contributing to SYNTH deterministic, safe, and rewarding.

---

## Purpose

Contributors are the engine of ecosystem growth. The contribution path must be clear, well-governed, and aligned with SYNTH's deterministic execution model.

---

## Deliverables

1. **CONTRIBUTING.md update** with Mission/Expedition workflow for external contributors.
2. **Good-first-issue labels** and a curated issue list.
3. **Pull request template** and review SLA.
4. **Contributor license agreement (CLA) or DCO process**.
5. **Contributor recognition page**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- A first-time contributor can go from issue to merged PR using only repository documentation.
- The good-first-issue backlog has at least 10 issues.
- Review SLA is published and tracked.
- CLA/DCO process is documented and enforced by CI.
- At least three external contributions are merged.

---

## Out of Scope

- Rewriting core governance.
- Bounty programs.
- Committing to merge any external PR without review.
