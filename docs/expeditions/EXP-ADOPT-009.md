# EXP-ADOPT-009 — Launch Campaign

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** II — Launch Assets  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Coordinate a public launch that converts awareness into adoption.

---

## Purpose

A launch is a controlled event. This expedition plans, executes, and measures the initial SYNTH launch across the platforms where developers gather.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Launch campaign plan** with timeline, channels, and owners.
2. **Product Hunt and Indie Hackers launch pages**.
3. **Coordinated social posts** for X, Bluesky, Mastodon, LinkedIn, Reddit, Hacker News, and Lobsters.
4. **Launch-day monitoring and response plan**.
5. **Post-launch retrospective report**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Campaign runs on the approved date across all listed channels.
- Launch posts link to the homepage, docs, and repository.
- Product Hunt page includes a demo video and install instructions.
- Response time to comments and questions is under 4 hours during the launch window.
- Retrospective includes metrics against the baseline from EXP-ADOPT-019.

---

## Out of Scope

- Paid advertising.
- Influencer partnerships.
- Launching before EXP-ADOPT-020 certification.
