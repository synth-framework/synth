# EXP-ADOPT-001 — Brand Presence

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** I — Platform Foundations  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Establish a consistent, credible SYNTH brand presence across all growth-facing platforms.

---

## Purpose

Before adoption, developers must recognize SYNTH and trust its identity. This expedition defines the brand voice, visual identity, and platform presence so every public touchpoint reinforces the same message: deterministic execution you can trust.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Brand voice and messaging guide** in `docs/brand/voice.md`.
2. **Visual identity kit** (logo, color palette, typography) under `docs/brand/assets`.
3. **Platform profile copy** for GitHub, npm, LinkedIn, X, Bluesky, Mastodon, Discord, and YouTube.
4. **Consistency checklist** mapping every platform profile to the brand guide.
5. **Definition of Done (DoD)** for all future public-facing brand assets.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Brand guide is approved, versioned, and discoverable from the repo.
- All listed platforms have profile copy that uses the approved voice.
- Visual assets render correctly in both light and dark modes.
- No platform profile contradicts the brand voice or constitutional principles.
- The DoD defines a review process and owner for future assets.

---

## Out of Scope

- Paid advertising.
- Homepage visual design (owned by EXP-PROGRAM-027).
- Community moderation policy.
- Merchandise or physical assets.
