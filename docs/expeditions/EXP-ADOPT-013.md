# EXP-ADOPT-013 — Conference Material

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** III — Developer Education  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Produce reusable conference material that demonstrates SYNTH in live settings.

---

## Purpose

Conference talks and workshops create high-trust evidence and reach concentrated developer audiences. Reusable material makes every submission consistent.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Slide deck template** aligned with the brand guide.
2. **Talk abstract and outline**: "Deterministic Execution with SYNTH".
3. **Live demo script** with a fallback video.
4. **Workshop materials**: repo, exercises, and facilitator guide.
5. **Speaker notes and timing**.
6. **Submission tracker** for calls for papers.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Deck is reusable for both 20- and 40-minute slots.
- Demo has a recorded fallback in case of network failure.
- Workshop can be completed in 90 minutes.
- Materials are published in the repository under `docs/talks`.
- At least one call for papers is submitted.

---

## Out of Scope

- Travel or sponsorship budgets.
- Paid speaking slots.
- Conference booth design.
