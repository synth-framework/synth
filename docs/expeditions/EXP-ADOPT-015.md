# EXP-ADOPT-015 — Content Calendar

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** IV — Community  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Operate a sustainable publishing cadence across docs, blog, social, and video channels.

---

## Purpose

Consistent presence builds trust without relying on one-off launches. This expedition defines the rhythm, ownership, and review workflow for all public content.

---

## Deliverables

1. **90-day rolling content calendar**.
2. **Channel-specific posting frequencies**.
3. **Content backlog** and idea intake process.
4. **Editorial workflow**: draft → review → schedule → publish.
5. **Metrics tie-in** to EXP-ADOPT-019.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Calendar covers docs articles, social posts, videos, and community events.
- Each content item has an owner, due date, and success metric.
- Review workflow includes brand and technical review.
- Calendar is updated weekly.
- At least one month of content is scheduled ahead.

---

## Out of Scope

- Real-time newsjacking.
- Content requiring paid promotion.
- External guest content without review.
