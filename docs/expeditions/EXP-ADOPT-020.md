# EXP-ADOPT-020 — Launch Certification

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** V — AI Ecosystem  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Certify that SYNTH is ready for public launch and that all gating expeditions are complete.

---

## Purpose

Launch is a governance event. Certification proves that evidence supports the launch decision and that no blocking defects remain.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Launch readiness checklist**.
2. **Pre-launch audit** covering install, docs, homepage, examples, brand, metrics, and community channels.
3. **Launch gate decision record**.
4. **Rollback and incident response plan**.
5. **Post-launch validation report**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- All Phase I and Phase II expeditions are accepted or explicitly waived.
- Launch checklist is signed off by the program authority.
- No P0/P1 defects block launch.
- Rollback plan is tested and documented.
- Post-launch report is published within 7 days.

---

## Out of Scope

- Launching without certification.
- Redefining launch criteria after failing the audit.
- Post-launch product development.
