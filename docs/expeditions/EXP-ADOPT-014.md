# EXP-ADOPT-014 — Social Media Assets

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** IV — Community  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Create a reusable set of on-brand social media assets for ongoing community engagement.

---

## Purpose

Consistent visual assets make every post recognizable and professional. This expedition separates asset creation from content scheduling so both can move independently.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Asset library** for X, Bluesky, Mastodon, LinkedIn, Reddit, and Discord.
2. **Templates** for announcements, tips, releases, and community highlights.
3. **Brand-safe meme and illustration guidelines**.
4. **Thumbnail and hero image variants**.
5. **Asset index and usage guide**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least 20 reusable assets are created.
- All assets follow the brand guide from EXP-ADOPT-001.
- Templates include accessible alt-text guidance.
- Asset index is discoverable by content maintainers.
- Assets render correctly at each platform's recommended resolution.

---

## Out of Scope

- Automated social posting bots.
- Engagement farming tactics.
- Paid social campaigns.
