# EXP-ADOPT-010 — Developer Outreach

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** III — Developer Education  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Build relationships with developers who are likely to adopt and advocate for SYNTH.

---

## Purpose

Direct outreach creates early evidence of product-market fit and surfaces real-world feedback before broad launch.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Target developer persona definitions**.
2. **Outreach playbook**: message templates, channels, and follow-up cadence.
3. **Seed user and early-adopter program**.
4. **Feedback capture process** tied to EXP-ADOPT-021.
5. **Contribution invitations** for interested developers.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- At least 20 seed users are identified and contacted.
- The early-adopter program has an onboarding path and a feedback mechanism.
- Outreach messages use the approved brand voice.
- Feedback is routed to the Community Listening & Feedback Loop.
- At least three independent pilots are started.

---

## Out of Scope

- Spam or unsolicited bulk email.
- Paid recruitment.
- Commitments to custom features for individual users.
