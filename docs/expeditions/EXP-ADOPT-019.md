# EXP-ADOPT-019 — Metrics

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** High  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** I — Platform Foundations  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Establish a trustworthy metrics baseline that measures meaningful adoption.

---

## Purpose

Without good metrics, growth activity becomes vanity. This expedition defines what to measure, how to collect it, who owns it, and how to act on it.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Metrics taxonomy** covering:
   - Repository stars
   - Contributors
   - Successful installations
   - Returning users
   - Missions executed
   - Documentation completion
   - Homepage completion
   - Skill downloads
   - SDK downloads
   - Community questions answered
   - Independent blog posts
   - Third-party integrations
   - AI agents capable of using SYNTH
2. **Data collection plan** for each metric: source, frequency, and owner.
3. **Dashboard or report** rendering the metrics.
4. **Privacy-preserving telemetry schema**.
5. **Review cadence and anomaly response process**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Every success metric from EXP-PROGRAM-037 has a definition, source, and owner.
- Dashboard updates at least weekly.
- Telemetry does not collect PII.
- Metrics are reviewed in a recurring governance meeting.
- Baseline is established before the launch campaign.

---

## Out of Scope

- Benchmarking against competitors.
- Public leaderboards that incentivize gaming.
- Metrics used as performance targets for individuals.
