# EXP-ADOPT-007 — Video Library

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** II — Launch Assets  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Produce a curated video library that accelerates developer onboarding and trust.

---

## Purpose

Video content reduces time-to-value and creates shareable evidence for social, conference, and homepage use.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Script and storyboard** for each video.
2. **At least three launch videos**: install SYNTH, create a Mission, explain replay.
3. **Closed captions and transcripts** for each video.
4. **Hosting plan** (YouTube) and playlist organization.
5. **Video embed integration** in docs.
6. **Accessibility compliance**: captions, contrast, and pacing.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each video is 10 minutes or less.
- Captions are accurate and synchronized.
- Videos are published on the official SYNTH YouTube channel.
- Docs site embeds videos without autoplay.
- At least one video demonstrates a full Mission lifecycle.

---

## Out of Scope

- Live streaming or webinar infrastructure.
- Paid video promotion.
- Animated explainers beyond the initial three videos.
