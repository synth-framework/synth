# EXP-ADOPT-011 — Community Programs

**Status:** Proposed  
**Kind:** Expedition  
**Priority:** Medium  
**Program:** EXP-PROGRAM-037 — Ecosystem Adoption & Community Growth  
**Phase:** III — Developer Education  
**Authority:** Synth Architectural Constitution

---

## Goal

Establish programs that turn users into active community members.

---

## Purpose

A healthy community scales support, creates content, and improves the product. This expedition defines the structure, events, and recognition systems that make participation worthwhile.

---

## Deliverables

1. **Community charter and code of conduct** for Discord.
2. **Onboarding flow** for new community members.
3. **Regular event cadence**: office hours, AMA, and show-and-tell sessions.
4. **Recognition program** for contributors and helpers.
5. **Moderation guidelines** and escalation path.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Discord server has a public invite, roles, and channels.
- Onboarding message explains SYNTH and how to ask questions.
- Event schedule is published at least one month ahead.
- Recognition program is documented and at least one cycle is completed.
- Code of conduct is visible and enforceable.

---

## Out of Scope

- Paid community management staffing.
- Off-platform community ownership.
- Formal governance votes from community members.
