Mission Studio

The interactive environment where a human idea becomes an accepted, evidence-backed outcome.

What Mission Studio does

The nine public steps of the SYNTH journey, all in one place.

1

Idea

The user describes what they want to build.

2

Questions

SYNTH asks for the clarity it needs.

3

Understanding

SYNTH presents what it understood for confirmation.

4

Contract

The user approves the boundary between understanding and building.

5

Mission

SYNTH derives the mission from the approved contract.

6

Plan

The mission becomes a set of executable plan items.

7

Evidence

Execution produces evidence that the work happened.

8

Review

The user compares the outcome to the contract.

9

Acceptance

The user accepts the outcome and the journey is complete.
