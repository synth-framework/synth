Mission Studio

The planning environment where ideas become approved missions.

What Mission Studio does

  1. Chart a mission. Capture the strategic goal.
  2. Gather evidence. Record what you know before deciding.
  3. Define expeditions. Break the mission into bounded pieces of work.
  4. Add objectives. Make each expedition concrete.
  5. Approve the plan. Commit an immutable snapshot.

What Mission Studio does not do

Mission Studio does not execute work. Execution happens through the approved plan, where every action is checked and recorded as an event.

Snapshot lineage

Every approval produces a new snapshot that references the previous one. You can review what changed, when, and why.
