The SYNTH workflow

Intent becomes Discovery. Discovery becomes Mission. Mission becomes Expedition. Every step produces evidence.

Intent

Capture what you want to build.

Discovery

Extract constraints, capabilities, and unknowns.

Mission

Approve a strategic goal with success criteria.

Expeditions

Plan bounded investigations and builds.

Governance

Validate and produce evidence.

Replay

Prove state matches the event history.

Repository

Materialize governed artifacts.

Seven public concepts

Synth is explained with exactly seven concepts. Everything else is implementation detail.

Mission

The strategic goal you are working toward.

Expedition

A bounded investigation or build that moves the mission forward.

Evidence

What you know and how confidently you know it.

Plan

The approved path forward, ready to execute.

Event

An immutable record that something happened.

State

The current picture, derived from events.

Replay

Rebuilding state from events to prove correctness.
